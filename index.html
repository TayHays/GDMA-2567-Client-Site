About Us

Endless Summer Surf School offers programs from complete beginners to advanced surfers of any age.

We teach you to surf at your own pace in a relaxed environment building your self-confidence in the water and the sport of surfing. We are dedicated to finding the best, fun and safest waist deep waves daily, perfect for progressing.

Only surf school with lesson permits in Manu and Whale Bay
